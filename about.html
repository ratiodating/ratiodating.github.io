About Us

Who we are as a company

We're a UCLA based startup that seeks to shake up the dating app industry by introducing an entirely new way of sorting through potential matches


Instead of merely indicating interest by right swiping on the other person, users sort three randomly generated people into catergories of "fling" (casual hookup), "ring" (more serious dating), and "ding" (no particular interest). From there users are able to DM each other and get the conversation going, with the extreme benefit of knowing exactly what the other person is looking for right off the bat. Not only is this a significantly more fun way of using dating apps, but it's considerably more efficient too


We're looking to find success at UCLA, USC, and other colleges in the general SoCal region, before raising pre-seed money, and taking this app national.
