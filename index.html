Frequently Asked Questions

What's your ultimate mission?

To redifine the way that people use dating apps, by 1) providing a more fun/enjoyable "swiping" experience and 2) consistently connecting users with other people that share their exact same relationship preferences for the first time ever

Do you have to pay to use Ratio?

Nope! Use of the app is completley free, and there are no slimy upcharges either. However, we do offer increased matches per day, algorithm boosts, and bonus features for tiered monthly subscriptions

How do you vet other users?

We require photo verification from every new person that joins our platform, and also heavily weight feedback from people that have met up in real life to ensure that all of our users are having the best experience possible
