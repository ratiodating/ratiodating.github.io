Timeline

Projected sequences of events

April 2023

Core Team Assembly

Finalizing seven total core team members (five new additions), as the sheer best of the best, after two rounds of interviews... final result is a special collection of fully devoted, ambitious individuals 

June 2023

Launch MVP on TestFlight

After two months of intense development, the beta version of Ratio gets launched on TestFlight, for UCLA students to hop on and test this new, exciting dating app startup

July 2023

Make Strong Brand Push

Once the beta version of the app has been pushed out, attention shifts to garnering hype and attention both at school and in the Greater Los Angeles County Region. Official Brand Ambassadors will be onboarded for merch photo shoots and to help aid the new marketing department in their endeavors

August 2023

Raise Pre-Seed Round

After two months of solid TestFlight traction, we plan to raise our pre-seed round ($500,000 for 10% equity) to be able to fully scale this startup via full time developers and a substantial marketing budget

September 2023

Official App Store Launch!

After validating our proof of concept, ironing out all the bugs, adding new features, gaining social traction, and growing a sizable social media following, we officially launch on the App store, with all of the pieces in place to reach the elusive state of exponential growth
